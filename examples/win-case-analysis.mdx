This example shows how to analyze a CSV of your closed deals to identify ICP patterns, extract common pain points, and build a lookalike campaign targeting similar companies.

## Scenario

You're building Extruct (company intelligence API) and you've closed 23 deals over the past 6 months. You want to analyze what these customers have in common and find more companies like them.

## Starting Prompt

```
I'm building www.extruct.ai.
Here's a list of my won deals [attach: won_deals.csv]
Analyze them and find similar companies to target.
```

**Attached CSV (`won_deals.csv`):**

```csv
company_name,domain,industry,employee_count,use_case,close_date,deal_value
SupplierIQ,supplieriq.io,Procurement Software,85,Vendor scoring,2025-01-15,2400

LeadEnrich,leadenrich.com,Sales Intelligence,230,Lead enrichment,2025-02-18,9600

... (23 total rows)
```

**Key learnings:**

1. **Hypothesis #1 (B2B Scoring) was strongest**: 14.5% reply rate, 48% of positive replies
2. **Company size matters**: 100-200 employee companies had 2x reply rate vs 20-50
3. **"API-forward" enrichment was predictive**: Grade 4-5 companies replied at 16.2% vs 7.8% for grade 2-3
4. **Recent funding = strong hook**: 19 of 25 positive replies were from companies with funding/product news in last 6 months
